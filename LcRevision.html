David Brown 1390

The David Brown 1390 is a utility tractor that was manufactured by J.I. Case in Meltham Mills, Huddersfield, England from 1980 to 1984. It is equipped with a 3.6 L four-cylinder diesel engine and a synchromesh with hydraulic dry disc clutch transmission with 12 forward and 4 reverse gears. The David Brown 1390 is available in both 2WD and 4WD configurations.
